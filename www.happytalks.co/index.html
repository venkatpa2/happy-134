Our Story

About Us

Connection is a basic human need

Experience the power of connection with Happy Talks.

As a seasoned professional with over 18 years in real estate sales and leadership, I have assisted hundreds of families and witnessed the profound impact that family dynamics can have on individuals. From assisting families with aging in place plans to finding multigenerational homes, I have been there every step of the way.

Throughout my journey, I have engaged in heartfelt conversations with countless individuals, witnessing the moment when realization dawns on a grandparent who must leave behind their beloved home, neighborhood, and daily routine. These experiences have taught me that families truly care, yet they are often overwhelmed by their busy lives.

That's why I created Happy Talks – a solution designed to foster connection for older adults, enriching their lives and improving their overall well-being. Our goal is to provide families with an easy-to-use platform that not only enables them to create more conversation starters with their loved ones, but also combat isolation.

At Happy Talks, our dedicated team is driven by the desire to make a genuine impact and serve others. We believe in transforming the lives of older adults, one individual at a time.

Our mission is to create an exceptional experience for you. Trust us to go above and beyond in providing the best service and products that reflect your love and care. Discover the transformative power of connection with Happy Talks today.
